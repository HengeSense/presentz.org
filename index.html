Presentz

Presentz is a javascript library for synchronizing videos and slides, so to reproduce conference talks as faithfully as possible.

 

It's a mashup, as it merges things like Vimeo, Youtube and Slideshare together. But you can use raw images and video files as well, unleashing the HTML5 video tag power.

How does it work?

 

First and longest, you need to record a video of a talk, cut it, synchronize the video and audio channels and everything a good TV editor would do. Yeah, it's scary, I know, but there are lots of good tools out there. I personally use Kdenlive: it's opensource and does its job.

 

Then download the editor and unpack it into the video file folder.
