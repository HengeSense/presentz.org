Presentz

Presentz is a javascript library for synchronizing videos and slides, so to reproduce conference talks as faithfully as possible.

 

It's a mashup, as it merges things like Vimeo, Youtube, Blip.tv and Slideshare together. But you can use raw images or swf and video files as well, unleashing the HTML5 video tag power.

 

Presentz is a work-in-progress. Soon there will be an editor, while more video and slide backends will be supported.

 

For now, you can join the mailing list and ask questions, take a look at the demo talks or enjoy the JUG Torino talks catalog.
